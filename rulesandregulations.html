Hostel Rules & Regulations

General Rules

  • All students must be formally admitted to the hostel before entering.
  • Students must adhere to all hostel rules and maintain discipline.
  • Each student is responsible for keeping their room clean and organized.
  • Use of electric appliances like stoves and irons is strictly prohibited.
  • Smoking, alcohol, and any form of substance abuse are strictly prohibited.
  • Hostel residents must return to the hostel by the designated curfew time.
  • Ragging and bullying are strictly prohibited and will result in strict disciplinary action.
  • Visitors are not allowed inside student rooms without prior permission.

Financial & Attendance Rules

  • All fees must be paid on time. Non-payment may result in expulsion.
  • Students must attend hostel meetings and comply with hostel regulations.
  • Damages to hostel property will be charged to the responsible student.
  • Leaving the hostel without prior permission from authorities is not allowed.

Safety & Security

  • Students must respect hostel staff and fellow residents at all times.
  • Loss of personal belongings is the student’s responsibility.
  • Hostel authorities hold the final decision in all matters related to hostel regulations.

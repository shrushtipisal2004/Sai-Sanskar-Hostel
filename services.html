Our Services

Dining Facility

We offer a hygienic and nutritious dining experience with a variety of meal options to cater to different tastes and dietary needs.

Laundry Service

Our on-site laundry service ensures that students have clean and fresh clothes at all times.

24/7 Security

Our hostel is equipped with CCTV cameras and security personnel to ensure a safe and secure environment for all residents.

High-Speed Wi-Fi

Stay connected with our high-speed Wi-Fi, available throughout the hostel premises.

Medical Assistance

We have a dedicated medical room and tie-ups with nearby hospitals for quick medical assistance.

Gym

A well-equipped gym is available for students to maintain their fitness and stay healthy.

Study Room

Dedicated study rooms with a peaceful environment for focused learning and group discussions.

Transport Service

Transport facilities are available to help students commute to nearby colleges and city areas.

CCTV Monitoring

All common areas are monitored 24/7 through CCTV cameras to ensure the safety of residents.

First Aid Kit

First aid kits are available on every floor to handle minor injuries and emergencies.
